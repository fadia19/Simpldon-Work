About Us

Who We Are ??


SIMPLDON is an online reservation community that provides multiple services to customers through a real time communication system that helps them to reserve anything they want whenever and wherever they are, as well these systems are installed at our services providers places and synchronized with our website in order to ease the reservation process for both customers that reserve online or from the services providers places.


Our Vision


Becoming the leading community for providing online reservations as well as becoming the brand for simplicity, cost and time efficiency in providing services with services providers and service consumers uniting at our platform towards a more efficient society.


Our Mission


Providing easy, instant, secure services through connecting customers with service providers saving cost, effort and -most importantly- time through dual channel continuously updated systems; Providing variety of multitude services through a unified platform.


What Do I Gain ??

  • If you are a customer

    • You will be provided with a safe, secure, user-friendly website and mobile application that provides multiple services and this will ease your reservations processes instead of wasting your time and effort going to the service provider's to reserve.
    • You will be able to see the available reservations for the upcoming 15 days.
    • You will be provided with an easy, secure and trustful online payment method that you can use 24/7.
    • You will discover more providers for your required services.
    • You will enjoy periodic offers and promotions.

  • If you are a Service Provider

    • You will be included into a community that includes similar and different service providers.
    • We are offering you a secure payment method, accordingly you will be able to take deposit from customers in order to confirm their reservations.
    • You will be able to reduce/eliminate the noise that you are facing through reservations with mobile/phone every day.
    • We will provide you with a service provider account, through which you will be able to monitor, handle and reserve at your place, Also you will be able to monitor your revenue statistics whenever and wherever you are.
    • We will market for your entity through our fan page and website during the entire contract validation date.
