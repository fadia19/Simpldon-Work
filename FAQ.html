FAQ

1-What is SIMPLDON ?


SIMPLDON is an online reservation community that provides multiple services to customers through a real time communication system that helps them to reserve anything they want whenever and wherever they are, as well these systems are installed at our services providers places and synchronized with our website in order to ease the reservation process for both customers that reserve online or from the services providers places.

We have started in September 2015 covering Egypt, Cairo;
Specifically:

  • Nasr City
  • Heliopolis
  • 5th Settelment
  • Maadi
  • 6th of October

2-Why Should I reserve using SIMPLDON instead of reserving using Mobile phone ?


  • You are provided with multiple services that you can reserve through one website instead of Remembering all your services providers number.
  • You are provided with each service provider schedule for the upcoming 15 days updated and synchronized through a real time communication system.
  • You are provided with Fawry as an easy, secured and trustful online payment method that you can use 24/7 through any Fawry's vendor.
  • You will not get a busy tune anymore.
  • You are provided with a receipt containing every detail about your reservation as a confirmation on reserving the service.

3-Is SIMPLDON secure ?


Yes, SIMPLDON uses the most up to date security systems to ensure that all of your personal details are protected.

4-Is it necessary to log in to SIMPLDON to reserve ?


Yes, you should log in so that you would have an account that will make you able to pay any reservations deposit online through fawry payment method.

5-How do I reserve through SIMPLDON ?


The whole process is Handled by SIMPLDON, the steps that you should follow for a correct reservation are:

  • Log in to SIMPLDON.
  • Choose the service you want to reserve.
  • Check the Deposit that you should pay.
  • Go to the nearest Fawry's vendor and choose online payment then SIMPLDON and at last choose the deposit amount you want to recharge with.
  • Take Fawry's receipt and save the pin code.
  • Recharge your account by entering Fawry's pin code on the balance button.
  • Reserve the required service.

6-How do I know if my reservation has been confirmed ?


Once you have reserved the required service, a confirmation pop-up with a download button for the receipt that you will show to the service provider.

7-If I forgot to download my receipt what should I do ?


Don't worry, go back to your home page you will find each reservation you have done on our website, At each reservation you will find a download my receipt button, you can download it whenever you won't.

8-Is there is any probability that two customers would reserve the same service at once ?


No, there is no probability for that as we have developed the website to be a real time communication system so once you reserve a specific time on a service the reservation will be closed for any other customer trying to reserve this specific time on this specific service even if the time difference between both is one second.

9-Is downloading my receipt the only way to confirm my reservation at the service provider place ?


No, If it was inapplicable to download the pdf version of the receipt, you can screenshot it but make sure that whole receipt is captured in the screenshot.

10-What would happen if I recharge with an amount which is higher than the reservation deposit amount ?


Directly, SIMPLDON will take the reservation amount only and the rest will still be in your balance.

11-What would happen if I recharge with an amount which is lower than the reservation deposit amount ?


While reserving the required service you will have a message containing " you should recharge your account with the required amount to reserve this service" and the reservation won't be accomplished.

12-Do you charge any fees for your service?


While paying at fawry's vendor, you will have to charge a very small additional fees for the transaction to be accomplished that will be mentioned before recharging through our website.

13-How long does it take to receive my receipt Through SIMPLDON's Website?


Instantaneously you will be provided with the confirmation pop-up and download the receipt button.

14-Can I exchange or refund my tickets after the order has been confirmed?


Yes, you can exchange or refund your tickets through SIMPLDON, but kindly check the Refund policy for the service you are about to reserve to make sure that it's valid to refund this reservation after that:

  • You will have to go to your home page.
  • View the reservation you want to cancel.
  • Press cancelling the reservation.
  • Choose whether you want to take the amount of deposit or to take it as a credit on your account.
    "In case you chose to take the amount of deposit you will have to follow the instructions that will be shown."
